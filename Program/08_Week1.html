    # Week 1: Scope and stage your project

    Meet your mentor! Get to know each other, review the value proposition you drafted and start thinking about your project goals and your goals for this program. Your mentor should get a general idea about the status and stage of the project, if you have a prototype or a product, if the project has a user base, among other things. Start thinking of assumptions and hypotheses underlying your project.

    # Goal of the week:

    • Define your value proposition clearly and start identifying your core hypotheses and assumptions, and status regarding validations

    Assignments:

    • Fill in the canvas the best you can try to be specific and concrete
    • Identify which aspects (technical and non-technical) in your project need further development and which have been sufficiently validated
